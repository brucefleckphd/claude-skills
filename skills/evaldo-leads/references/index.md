## The 10 Questions (Leads Answer First 6)

The lead section of a VSL answers:
1. What is the new opportunity?
2. Why now?
3. What's in it for me?
4. How does it work? (tease)
5. Who are you and why should I listen?
6. Can you prove it? (initial proof)

The body/close answers:
7. How is this different?
8. What do I get?
9. What does it cost?
10. What do I do now?

## The Most Important Truth

**The lead does 80% of the selling.**

By the end of the lead, they should:
- Be emotionally engaged
- Believe you have something real
- Want what you're offering
- Trust you to deliver

The pitch just makes it official.
