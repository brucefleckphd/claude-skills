# Visual Metaphor Quick Reference

> Complete reference for mechanism visualization.

---

## The 8 Metaphor Categories

### 1. Body-as-System
**Core Idea:** Body parts as workers, departments, cities

**Examples:**
- "Your gut has tiny security guards"
- "Cells like factory workers"
- "Your liver is the body's water treatment plant"

**Best For:** Health, supplements, medical

---

### 2. Warfare
**Core Idea:** Enemies, battles, defenses

**Examples:**
- "Toxins invading your bloodstream"
- "Immune cells as soldiers"
- "Your body is under attack"

**Best For:** Threats, urgency, fear-based mechanisms

---

### 3. Container
**Core Idea:** Filling, overflowing, clogging

**Examples:**
- "Your toxin bucket is overflowing"
- "Fat cells filling up like storage tanks"
- "Clogged arteries like blocked pipes"

**Best For:** Accumulation problems, threshold issues

---

### 4. Journey
**Core Idea:** Paths, obstacles, destinations

**Examples:**
- "The road to health"
- "Roadblocks on your weight loss journey"
- "Finding the path others missed"

**Best For:** Transformation, progress, hope

---

### 5. Mechanical
**Core Idea:** Switches, engines, thermostats

**Examples:**
- "Flip the fat-burning switch"
- "Your metabolic engine is sputtering"
- "Reset your body's thermostat"

**Best For:** Control, on/off solutions, systems

---

### 6. Natural
**Core Idea:** Growth, seasons, gardens

**Examples:**
- "Your gut garden overrun with weeds"
- "Prune the dead weight"
- "Spring cleaning for your cells"

**Best For:** Organic, gentle, natural solutions

---

### 7. Financial
**Core Idea:** Money, debt, investment, ROI

**Examples:**
- "You're paying an invisible tax on your health"
- "Energy bankruptcy"
- "Metabolic debt"

**Best For:** Business, finance, efficiency

---

### 8. Time
**Core Idea:** Clocks, windows, seasons, timelines

**Examples:**
- "The metabolic window closing"
- "Your biological clock"
- "Time is working against you"

**Best For:** Urgency, FOMO, seasonal/cyclical mechanisms

---

## Domain-Specific Guidance

### Health/Supplements
✅ Best: Body-as-System, Warfare, Container, Natural
❌ Avoid: Financial (unless explicitly about health costs)

### Business/Marketing
✅ Best: Financial, Mechanical, Journey, Warfare
❌ Avoid: Heavily medical language

### Technology/AI
✅ Best: Mechanical, Warfare, Time, Journey
❌ Avoid: Organic/gardening metaphors

### Self-Help/Psychology
✅ Best: Journey, Time, Natural, Mechanical
❌ Avoid: Cold/clinical tone

---

## Visual Metaphor Scoring

Rate each visual metaphor 1-10:
- **Concreteness:** Can person visualize this?
- **Uniqueness:** Fresh or clichéd?
- **Domain Fit:** Matches market/product?
- **Emotional Hook:** Triggers reaction?
- **Shareability:** Easy to explain to others?

---

*High-scoring metaphors get integrated into copy, mechanisms, and supporting materials.*
