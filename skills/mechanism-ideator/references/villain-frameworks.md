# Villain Framework Quick Reference

> Complete reference for enemy construction in mechanism copy.

---

## The 10 Villain Archetypes

### 1. Hidden Enemy
**Core Concept:** Unknown cause lurking in plain sight

**Formula:** "The [hidden thing] you never knew was [damaging action]"

**Example:** Lectins - "These 'healthy' plant proteins are actually tearing holes in your gut lining"

**Best Markets:** Health, supplements

---

### 2. Betrayer
**Core Concept:** Trusted thing turned against you

**Formula:** "The [trusted thing] that's actually [betraying action]"

**Example:** "Your own immune system attacking your joints"

**Best Markets:** Health, relationships

---

### 3. Blocker
**Core Concept:** Something preventing success

**Formula:** "The [blocker] that's keeping you from [goal]"

**Example:** "Leptin resistance blocking your fat-burning signals"

**Best Markets:** Weight loss, business

---

### 4. Saboteur
**Core Concept:** Active undermining of efforts

**Formula:** "While you [effort], [saboteur] is undoing your work"

**Example:** "Cortisol sabotaging your sleep and storing belly fat"

**Best Markets:** Fitness, productivity

---

### 5. Conspiracy
**Core Concept:** System designed against them

**Formula:** "[Authority figures] don't want you to know about [truth]"

**Example:** "Big Pharma profits from keeping you sick"

**Best Markets:** Health, finance (use carefully)

---

### 6. Deficiency
**Core Concept:** Missing critical element

**Formula:** "You're missing the [element] your body desperately needs"

**Example:** "The forgotten mineral 80% of Americans are deficient in"

**Best Markets:** Supplements, health

---

### 7. Outdated System
**Core Concept:** Old approach no longer works

**Formula:** "[Old method] worked before, but [new condition] changed everything"

**Example:** "Calorie counting worked in the 1950s - before processed foods"

**Best Markets:** Diet, business, technology

---

### 8. Environmental Mismatch
**Core Concept:** World changed but they didn't adapt

**Formula:** "Modern [environment] doesn't match your [ancient biology]"

**Example:** "Your caveman body in a 2024 food environment"

**Best Markets:** Health, fitness, technology

---

### 9. Silent Accumulator
**Core Concept:** Building up unnoticed until it's too late

**Formula:** "[Bad thing] is accumulating in your [system] right now"

**Example:** "Microplastics accumulating in your organs"

**Best Markets:** Health, environmental, urgency-based

---

### 10. Invisible Tax
**Core Concept:** Hidden ongoing cost nobody talks about

**Formula:** "You're paying a hidden [tax] on every [action]"

**Example:** "The attention tax of multitasking"

**Best Markets:** Productivity, business, mental health

---

## The 7 Attack Patterns

1. **The Reveal** - "What they've been missing"
2. **The Betrayal** - "What they trusted is hurting them"
3. **The Accumulation** - "What's building up unnoticed"
4. **The Mismatch** - "Why their efforts don't work anymore"
5. **The Theft** - "What's being stolen from them"
6. **The Sabotage** - "What's working against their efforts"
7. **The Conspiracy** - "Why nobody's told them the truth"

---

## Villain Origin Story Template

Every villain needs:
1. **What it is** - The specific enemy
2. **Why it exists** - Origin/cause
3. **Why they don't see it** - Hidden, normalized, trusted
4. **How it attacks** - Specific mechanism of harm
5. **What it steals** - Results, health, time, money
6. **Who profits from it** - Who benefits from them NOT knowing?

---

## Naming Your Villain

**Strong Villain Names:**
- Specific, not vague
- Emotionally resonant (easy to hate)
- Memorable (3-4 syllables)
- Shareable ("I'm fighting the ___")

**Examples:**
- The Leptin Trap
- Paradigm Lock
- The Productivity Thief
- Silent Inflammation
- The Knowledge Ceiling
