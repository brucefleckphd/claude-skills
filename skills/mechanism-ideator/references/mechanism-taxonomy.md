# Mechanism Taxonomy Reference

> Complete reference for mechanism classification.

---

## Three-Component Structure

### UMP (Unique Mechanism Problem)
The hidden root cause or villain that's really causing their problem.

**Key Elements:**
- Names a specific, hidden enemy
- Explains WHY other solutions fail
- Creates "aha moment" of understanding
- Shifts blame away from the prospect

### UMS (Unique Mechanism Solution)
Why YOUR approach uniquely solves the root cause.

**Key Elements:**
- Directly addresses the UMP
- Explains the scientific/logical fix
- Shows why your method works differently
- Creates ownership of the solution path

### UMD (Unique Mechanism Delivery)
The named, memorable system/method/protocol.

**Key Elements:**
- Catchy, memorable name
- Easy to share and reference
- Implies proprietary methodology
- Becomes the "thing" they buy

## Villain Archetypes

1. **Hidden Enemy** - Unknown cause (lectins, toxins)
2. **Betrayer** - Trusted thing gone bad (friendly fire)
3. **Blocker** - Something preventing success
4. **Saboteur** - Active undermining
5. **Conspiracy** - System designed against them
6. **Deficiency** - Missing critical element
7. **Outdated System** - Old approach failing now
8. **Environmental Mismatch** - Modern world problem
9. **Silent Accumulator** - Building up unnoticed
10. **Invisible Tax** - Hidden ongoing cost
