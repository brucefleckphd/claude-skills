## Proof Hierarchy

From least to most powerful:
1. Claims (weakest)
2. Statistics/Data
3. Expert endorsement
4. Demonstrations
5. Customer testimonials
6. Case studies
7. Personal story with results (strongest)

Multiple proof types combined = undeniable belief.

## The Most Important Truth

**Stack proof until doubt is impossible.**

One proof point is a claim. Three is a pattern. Seven is undeniable. The body builds belief through accumulation.
