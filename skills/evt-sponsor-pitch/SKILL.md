# Create Sponsor Pitch

Turn your event into a sponsorship opportunity. This skill creates compelling sponsor packages that get funded.

## When to Use This Skill

- Seeking event sponsors
- Creating partnership proposals
- Building media kits
- Monetizing community events

## Required Inputs

- Event details and audience
- Sponsorship tier ideas
- Unique assets to offer
- Pricing goals
- Target sponsors

## Output Format

```markdown
# Sponsorship Prospectus: [Event Name]

**Event Date:** [Date]
**Expected Attendance:** [Number]
**Location:** [Where]

---

## The Opportunity

[Why this event matters and why sponsors should care]

---

## Audience Profile

**Who Attends:**
- [Demographic 1]: [%]
- [Demographic 2]: [%]

**Purchasing Power:** [Relevant stats]
**Engagement Level:** [Evidence of engaged audience]

---

## Sponsorship Packages

### Title Sponsor - $[Price]
*Limited to 1*

**Benefits:**
- Logo placement: [Everywhere]
- Speaking opportunity
- Attendee list access
- VIP experience
- [Unique benefit]

### Gold Sponsor - $[Price]
*Limited to 3*

**Benefits:**
- Logo placement: [Specific locations]
- Exhibit space
- Social mentions
- [Benefits]

### Silver Sponsor - $[Price]

**Benefits:**
- Logo placement
- [Benefits]

---

## À La Carte Options

| Opportunity | Investment | Details |
|-------------|------------|---------|
| Branded [X] | $[Price] | [Description] |
| [Activation] | $[Price] | [Description] |

---

## Past Sponsors

[Logos or names of previous sponsors]

---

## Contact

[Name]
[Email]
[Phone]
```
