# Design a Workshop

Create workshops that engage and transform. This skill produces complete facilitation guides with exercises and timing.

## When to Use This Skill

- Training sessions
- Team workshops
- Client strategy sessions
- Community events
- Educational programs

## Required Inputs

- Workshop topic and objectives
- Duration available
- Participant profiles
- Desired outcomes
- Format (in-person/virtual)

## Output Format

```markdown
# Workshop Design: [Workshop Name]

**Duration:** [Length]
**Participants:** [Number] [Profile]
**Format:** [In-person/Virtual/Hybrid]
**Facilitator:** [Name]

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this workshop, participants will:
1. [Objective 1 - know/understand]
2. [Objective 2 - be able to do]
3. [Objective 3 - feel/believe]

---

## Pre-Work (Optional)

**Assigned:** [X days before]
- [Pre-work item 1]
- [Pre-work item 2]

---

## Materials Needed

**Physical:**
- [Material 1]
- [Material 2]

**Digital:**
- [Tool/Link 1]
- [Tool/Link 2]

---

## Agenda

- Welcome and introductions

- Icebreaker: [Activity name]

### Section 1: [Topic] (X min)

**Content (X min):**
[Teaching points or presentation]

**Activity (X min):**
[Interactive exercise]

**Debrief (X min):**
[Discussion questions]

### Section 2: [Topic] (X min)

**Content (X min):**
[Teaching points]

[Exercise description]
- Step 1: [Instruction]

- Step 3: [Instruction]

[Questions to ask]

### Break (X min)

### Section 3: [Topic] (X min)
[Same format]

## Facilitation Notes

## Follow-Up
```
