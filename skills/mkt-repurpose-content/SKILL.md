# Repurpose Content

Turn one idea into ten pieces of content. This skill creates comprehensive repurposing plans that maximize your content investment.

## When to Use This Skill

- After creating hero content (podcast, video, blog)
- Content audit revealing underutilized assets
- Scaling content output without more creation time
- Cross-platform expansion

## Required Inputs

- Source content (URL, transcript, or text)
- Original format and platform
- Target platforms for repurposing
- Time/resource constraints
