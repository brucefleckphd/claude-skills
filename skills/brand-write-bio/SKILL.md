# Write Professional Bio

Tell your story compellingly. This skill creates bios that position you as the expert you are.

## When to Use This Skill

- Speaker submissions
- Website about pages
- Social media profiles
- Media kits
- Conference materials

## Required Inputs

- Current role and company
- Key achievements and credentials
- Target audience for bio
- Desired length (short/medium/long)
- Tone preference (formal/conversational)
- Notable clients, results, or recognition

## Output Format

```markdown
# Professional Bio Package: [Name]

---

## Long Bio (250-300 words)
*For website, speaker pages, formal materials*

[Third person, comprehensive bio covering background, achievements, philosophy, and personal touch]

---

## Medium Bio (100-150 words)
*For conference programs, guest posts, podcast introductions*

[Condensed version with key highlights]

---

## Short Bio (50-75 words)
*For social media, brief mentions, bylines*

[Punchy version with essential info]

---

## One-Liner
*For quick introductions, Twitter bio, etc.*

[15-20 word positioning statement]

---

## LinkedIn Headline Options

1. [Option 1 - Results-focused]
2. [Option 2 - Role-focused]
3. [Option 3 - Value-focused]

---

## LinkedIn About Section

[First person, conversational, with clear CTA]

---

## Twitter/X Bio

[160 characters max, with personality]

---

## Boilerplate Intro (For Others to Read)

"Please welcome [Name], [brief description]. [Key achievement]. [Topic they'll discuss]."
```
