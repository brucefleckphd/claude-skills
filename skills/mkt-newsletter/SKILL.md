# Write Newsletter

Create newsletters people actually want to read. This skill produces engaging, valuable newsletter content that builds relationships.

## When to Use This Skill

- Weekly newsletter creation
- Monthly roundups
- Special announcements
- Curated content digests

## Required Inputs

- Newsletter frequency and format
- Topics/themes to cover
- Any links or resources to include
- Primary CTA for this issue
- Audience insights (what's resonating)

## Output Format

```markdown
# Newsletter: [Issue Title]

**Issue Number:** [X]
**Send Date:** [Date]
**Theme:** [Theme]

---

## Subject Line Options
1. [Subject line A]
2. [Subject line B]
3. [Subject line C]

**Preview Text:** [Preview]

---

## Newsletter Body

### Opening Hook
[2-3 sentences that immediately engage - story, stat, or question]

---

### Main Content Section: [Topic]

[Your primary value-add content - insight, teaching, or story]

**Key Takeaway:** [One-liner they'll remember]

---

### Quick Wins / Tips

**Tip 1:** [Brief actionable tip]
**Tip 2:** [Brief actionable tip]
**Tip 3:** [Brief actionable tip]

---

### What I'm Reading/Using/Watching
[1-3 curated recommendations with brief context]

---

### From the Community
[Member wins, questions answered, or social proof]

---

### CTA Section

[Bridge to your ask]

**[CTA Button Text]**

---

### Sign-Off

[Personal closing that matches your voice]

[Your name]

P.S. [Bonus content or secondary CTA]
```
