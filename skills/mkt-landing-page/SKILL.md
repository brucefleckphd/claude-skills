# Write Landing Page Copy

Create landing pages that convert visitors to leads and customers. This skill produces conversion-optimized copy for any offer.

## When to Use This Skill

- New offer or product launch
- Lead magnet pages
- Webinar registration pages
- Sales pages
- Squeeze pages

## Required Inputs

- Offer description
- Target audience
- Main benefit/promise
- Key features
- Objections to overcome
- Social proof available

## Output Format

```markdown
# Landing Page Copy: [Offer Name]

**Offer Type:** [Lead Magnet/Webinar/Product/Service]
**Target:** [Audience segment]
**Primary Goal:** [Conversion action]

---

## Above the Fold

**Headline Options:**
1. [Headline - benefit-focused]
2. [Headline - curiosity-driven]
3. [Headline - problem-aware]

**Subheadline:**
[Supporting statement that clarifies the headline]

**Hero CTA:**
Button: [CTA text]
Supporting: [Microcopy under button]

---

## Problem Section

**Section Headline:** [Agitate the pain]

[2-3 sentences describing the problem your audience faces]

**Pain Points:**
- [Pain 1]
- [Pain 2]
- [Pain 3]

---

## Solution Section

**Section Headline:** [Introduce your solution]

[Paragraph explaining what you offer and how it solves the problem]

---

## Benefits Section

**Section Headline:** Here's What You'll Get

**Benefit 1:** [Benefit headline]
[Supporting description of this benefit]

**Benefit 2:** [Benefit headline]
[Supporting description]

**Benefit 3:** [Benefit headline]
[Supporting description]

---

## Social Proof Section

**Testimonial 1:**
> "[Quote]"
> — [Name], [Title/Company]

**Testimonial 2:**
> "[Quote]"
> — [Name], [Title/Company]

**Trust Badges/Logos:**
- [As seen in / Trusted by]

---

## What's Included Section

**Section Headline:** Everything You Get

| Component | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| [Item 1] | [$X value] |
| [Item 2] | [$X value] |
| [Item 3] | [$X value] |
| **Total Value** | **$[X]** |
| **Your Investment** | **$[Price]** |

---

## FAQ Section

**Q: [Common question 1]**
A: [Answer that overcomes objection]

**Q: [Common question 2]**
A: [Answer]

**Q: [Common question 3]**
A: [Answer]

---

## Risk Reversal

**Guarantee Headline:** [Your guarantee]

[Explanation of guarantee that removes risk]

---

## Final CTA Section

**Headline:** [Urgency or summary statement]

**CTA Button:** [Action text]

**Below Button:** [Final reassurance or scarcity element]
```
