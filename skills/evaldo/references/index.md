## Crown Jewels

### 1. The One Belief Formula
> "This new opportunity is the key to their desire and it's only attainable through my new mechanism."

### 2. The 10 Questions
1. What is the new opportunity?
2. Why now?
3. What's in it for me?
4. How does it work?
5. Who are you and why should I listen?
6. Can you prove it?
7. How is this different?
8. What do I get?
9. What's it gonna cost?
10. What do I do now?

### 3. The Croc Brain Filters
- Lion/Rabbit (threat or opportunity?)
- Geico (simple or complex?)
- Curiosity (new or familiar?)

### 4. The Three Box Check
- Emotional (does it move you?)
- Simple (can a child understand?)
- New (never seen before?)

### 5. The DICK Method
- Disrupt
- Intrigue
- Call to action
- Keep short

## Quick Workflow

**Idea → Structure → Write → Evaluate**

1. Three Box Check → Croc Brain → One Belief
2. 10 Questions → Story Type → Credibility
3. Headline → Lead → Body → Bullets → Close
4. Check Croc Brain → Verify 10 Questions → Test Belief

## Decision Trees

### Which Element Skill?

**Planning Stage:**
- Core idea development → `evaldo-ideas`
- Mindset/filters → `evaldo-philosophy`

**Writing Stage:**
- Headlines → `evaldo-headlines`
- Opening/leads → `evaldo-leads`
- Body/proof → `evaldo-body`
- Bullets → `evaldo-bullets`
- Close/pitch → `evaldo-close`

**Traffic Stage:**
- Email creative → `evaldo-lifts`

### Which Story Type?

- You have real credentials → Guru
- You were like the prospect → Little Guy
- Market has shared frustration → Common Enemy
