## Common Workflows

### Complete Close Sequence
1. close-offer-structure (plan all 7 components)
2. close-price-minimization (make price feel small)
3. close-bonus-strategy (stack bonuses)
4. close-guarantee-presentation (remove risk)
5. close-scarcity-framework (create urgency)
6. close-future-pace (paint vivid future)
7. close-crossroads (force decision)
8. close-exact-instructions (remove friction)

### Guarantee Writing
1. close-guarantee-presentation (positive language, confidence)
2. close-risk-transfer (transfer all risk)
3. close-mom-test (authenticity check)

### Bonus Creation
1. close-bonus-strategy (fill gaps)
2. close-double-purpose-bonus (presuppose success)

### Final CTA Optimization
1. close-exact-instructions (step-by-step)
2. close-passion (add energy)
3. close-mom-test (authenticity check)

### Extended Close
1. close-crossroads (main close)
2. close-responsibility-transfer (transfer ownership)
3. close-faq-extension (handle questions)
