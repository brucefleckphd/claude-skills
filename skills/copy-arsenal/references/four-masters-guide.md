- `clayton-structure` - 11-component architecture
- `clayton-testing` - Split testing
- `clayton-business` - Business economics
- `clayton-events` - Event copy

**Best For:**
- Health and supplement markets
- Financial newsletters
- Markets requiring heavy proof
- Extreme-skeptic audiences (burned before, trust issues)
