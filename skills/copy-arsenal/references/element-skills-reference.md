# Element Skills Complete Reference

## What Are Element Skills?

Element skills are surgical tools for improving specific sections of copy. Instead of running a full master workflow, you target exactly what needs fixing.

**When to use:** You have existing copy and one section needs improvement.
**When NOT to use:** You're writing from scratch (use the master skill instead).

## Cross-Master Element Comparison

### Headlines
| Master | Style | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Clayton | Emotional triggers, fear/greed/rage | Health, financial, emotional markets |
| Carlton | Incongruous juxtaposition, stopping power | Bold brands, attention-starved markets |
| Deutsch | 100 Headlines Method, sophistication | Educated audiences, premium offers |
| Evaldo | 13 types, croc brain activation | VSLs, long-form, mechanism-driven |

### Leads/Openings
| Master | Style | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Clayton | 24 strategies by awareness level | Any awareness level (most versatile) |
| Carlton | Story-driven, hook-heavy | Personality brands, bold openings |
| Deutsch | Dual journey, resistance-free | Sophisticated audiences, high-end |
| Evaldo | 10 Questions structure | VSLs, long-form educational |

### Bullets
| Master | Style | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Clayton | Fascinations, intrigue | Health, financial teasers |
| Carlton | One-Two Punch, power | Bold, impactful bullet lists |
| Deutsch | Polished specificity | Premium offers, educated readers |
| Evaldo | Mechanism intrigue | VSLs, mechanism-driven products |

### Closes
| Master | Style | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Clayton | 10-step sequence, nuclear guarantee | Skeptical markets, heavy proof |
| Carlton | Takeaway, bold | Personality brands, confident sells |
| Deutsch | Permission-based, gentle | Educated audiences, high-ticket |
| Evaldo | Stack + urgency + price anchoring | VSLs, webinar scripts |
