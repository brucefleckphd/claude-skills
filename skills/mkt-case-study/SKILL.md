# Build a Case Study

Turn client wins into your best sales collateral. This skill creates compelling, story-driven case studies that demonstrate real results.

## When to Use This Skill

- After major client success
- Sales team needs proof points
- Website needs social proof
- Proposal attachment creation
- Conference presentation prep

## Required Inputs

**Client Information:**
- Company name (and permission status)
- Industry and company size
- Contact who can provide quotes

**Project Details:**
- Challenge they faced before
- Solution you provided
- Implementation timeline
- Results achieved (with numbers)
