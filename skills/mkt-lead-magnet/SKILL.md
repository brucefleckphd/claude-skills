# Create Lead Magnet

Design irresistible lead magnets that grow your email list. This skill creates high-value resources that attract your ideal audience.

## When to Use This Skill

- Building email list from scratch
- Creating content upgrades
- Generating webinar leads
- New funnel development

## Required Inputs

- Target audience and their pain points
- Format preference (checklist, guide, template, etc.)
- Topic area
- How this connects to paid offerings

## Output Format

```markdown
# Lead Magnet Blueprint: [Title]

**Format:** [Checklist/Guide/Template/Toolkit/Swipe File]
**Target Audience:** [Who this is for]
**Primary Problem Solved:** [Pain point addressed]
**Perceived Value:** [High/Medium]
**Time to Create:** [Estimate]
```
