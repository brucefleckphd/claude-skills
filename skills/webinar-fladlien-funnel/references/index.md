## The Attrition Math

**Example funnel (F5):**

| Stage | Number | % of Previous | Cumulative % |
|-------|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Email list | 50,000 | - | 100% |
| Opens email | 10,000 | 20% | 20% |
| Clicks link | 2,000 | 20% | 4% |
| Registers | 1,000 | 50% | 2% |
| Attends live | 500 | 50% | 1% |
| At close | 200 | 40% | 0.4% |
| Purchases | 20 | 10% | 0.04% |

**Key Insight:** Improving ANY stage improves final number.

Example: Improving show rate from 50% to 60%:
- 600 attend instead of 500 (+100)
- 240 at close instead of 200 (+40)
- 24 sales instead of 20 (+4 = 20% revenue increase!)

Small improvements compound.
