# Evaldo Close

Evaldo Albuquerque's close frameworks. The close is where everything converts. All the work in lead and body leads here. This is Questions 8-10 of the 10 Questions.

## When to Use This Skill

- Writing VSL close/pitch section
- Presenting the offer
- Price anchoring and stacking
- Building urgency
- Final objection handling
- Guarantee presentation

## Crown Jewels

1. **The Stack** - Building perceived value systematically
2. **Price Anchoring** - Making the price feel small
3. **The Risk Reversal** - Making the guarantee sell
4. **The Urgency Sequence** - Creating action now

## Complete Workflow

1. **Reveal the offer** - What they get
2. **Stack value** - Build perceived worth
3. **Anchor price** - Compare to alternatives
4. **Drop the price** - The actual investment
5. **Present bonuses** - Additional value
6. **Guarantee** - Remove all risk
7. **Urgency** - Why act now
8. **CTA** - What to do
9. **Re-close** - Second pass

## Critical Evaldo Quote

> "The close is where everything pays off. If you've done your job in
> the lead and body, the close is just making it official. But still,
> close hard. You've earned the right."
