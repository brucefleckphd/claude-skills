# Build Content Strategy

Transform scattered posting into strategic content. This skill creates quarterly content strategies with themes, pillars, and distribution plans.

## When to Use This Skill

- Quarterly content planning
- New product/service launch
- Brand refresh or pivot
- Content audit and reset
- New channel expansion

## Required Inputs

**Business Context:**
- Current quarter goals
- Target audience segments
- Products/services to promote
- Competitive positioning

**Content Audit:**
- What's working now (top performing content)
- What's not working
- Gaps to fill

**Resources:**
- Content creation capacity
- Channels you can realistically maintain
- Budget for production/promotion

## Instructions

1. **Define content mission** - Why does your content exist?
2. **Establish pillars** - 3-5 core themes you own
3. **Map to buyer journey** - Content for each stage
4. **Create calendar** - Tactical posting schedule
5. **Set KPIs** - How you'll measure success
