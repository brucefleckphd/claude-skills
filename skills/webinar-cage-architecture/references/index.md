## The 4-Act Structure Breakdown

**Act 1: Introduction (<2 minutes)**
- Hook Formulas apply here
- Deliver on Title promise
- Establish Authority
- Set expectations

**Act 2: Stick Strategies (2-5 minutes)**
- Keep them from leaving
- Build momentum
- Transition to content

**Act 3: Core Content (30-45 minutes)**
- 5-10 Chunks (Chunk Architecture)
- Stories supporting each chunk (Story Arsenal)
- Teases creating forward momentum (Tease-Don't-Tell)
- Sticks at transitions (Stick Strategies)

**Act 4: Close (5-10 minutes)**
- Designed in Layer 4 (Conversion)

## Timing Guidelines

- **Total presentation:** 40-60 minutes
- **Act 1:** <2 minutes
- **Act 2:** 2-5 minutes
- **Act 3:** 30-45 minutes (5-7 min per chunk × 5-10 chunks)
- **Act 4:** 5-10 minutes

## Quality Standard

Architecture is complete when:
- [ ] 4-Act Structure is clearly mapped with timing
- [ ] 5-10 Chunks are designed with teaching points
- [ ] Each Chunk has a supporting Story
- [ ] Hook is crafted for Act 1
- [ ] Sticks are placed at all critical transitions
- [ ] 5-7 Tease moments are woven throughout
- [ ] Content delivers on Title promise
- [ ] Everything traces back to Desire Tap
