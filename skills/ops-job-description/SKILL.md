# Write Job Description

Attract the right candidates with compelling job posts. This skill creates role descriptions that filter for culture fit and capability.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating new positions
- Updating existing job posts
- Contractor/freelancer briefs
- Internal role definitions
- Succession planning

## Required Inputs

- Role title and type (FT/PT/Contract)
- Department and reporting structure
- Core responsibilities
- Required qualifications
- Company culture elements
- Compensation range

## Output Format

```markdown
# Job Description: [Role Title]

**Department:** [Department]
**Reports To:** [Title]
**Location:** [Remote/Hybrid/On-site]
**Type:** [Full-time/Part-time/Contract]
**Compensation:** [Range]

---

## About [Company Name]

[1-2 paragraphs about your company, mission, and culture]

---

## The Opportunity

[What makes this role exciting and impactful]

---

## What You'll Do

**Core Responsibilities:**
- [Responsibility 1]
- [Responsibility 2]
- [Responsibility 3]
- [Responsibility 4]
- [Responsibility 5]

**Day-to-Day:**
[Specific activities they'll perform regularly]

---

## What You'll Bring

**Required:**
- [Years] of experience in [field]
- [Skill 1]
- [Skill 2]
- [Skill 3]

**Preferred:**
- [Nice-to-have 1]
- [Nice-to-have 2]

**Mindset:**
- [Cultural attribute 1]
- [Cultural attribute 2]
- [Cultural attribute 3]

---

## What We Offer

- [Benefit 1]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]
- [Growth opportunity]

---

## How to Apply

[Application instructions and any requirements like portfolio, cover letter, etc.]

---

## Hiring Process

1. [Step 1 - Application review]
2. [Step 2 - Initial screen]
3. [Step 3 - Interview/Assessment]
4. [Step 4 - Final decision]

---

*[Company Name] is an equal opportunity employer.*
```
