# Create Social Content

Transform ideas into scroll-stopping social content. This skill creates platform-optimized posts that engage your audience.

## When to Use This Skill

- Batch content creation
- Repurposing long-form content
- Engagement post ideas
- Thought leadership posts
- Promotional content

## Required Inputs

- Topic or source content
- Platform(s) to target
- Content goal (engage, educate, convert)
- Brand voice guidelines
- Hashtag preferences
