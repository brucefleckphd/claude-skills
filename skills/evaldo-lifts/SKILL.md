# Evaldo Lifts

Evaldo Albuquerque's lift note and email creative frameworks. Lift notes are the emails that drive traffic to VSLs - they're supplementary creative pieces that extend the reach of a promotion.

## When to Use This Skill

- Writing emails that drive to VSL
- Creating supplementary creative
- Testing different angles
- Email campaign planning
- DICK method for short-form

## Crown Jewels

1. **The DICK Method** - Disrupt, Intrigue, Call to Action, Keep short
2. **Safe vs Intriguing** - Two lift strategies
3. **Lift Angle Variety** - Different hooks for same VSL
4. **Campaign Architecture** - Sequence structure

## Complete Workflow

1. **Plan angles** - What hooks will work?
2. **Choose strategy** - Safe or intriguing?
3. **Apply DICK** - Structure the email
4. **Write subject line** - Get the open
5. **Create body** - Drive the click
6. **Transition to VSL** - Seamless handoff

## Critical Evaldo Quote

> "The lift note's job is simple: get them to the VSL. That's it.
> DICK method - Disrupt, Intrigue, Call to action, Keep it short.
> Every element serves the click."
