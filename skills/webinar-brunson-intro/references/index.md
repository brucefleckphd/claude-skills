## Master System (Tier 2)

### The Bold Promise Ruler
**Node:** 3.1 Hook Architecture
**Core Focus:** Set singular measurement standard for entire webinar
**When to Use:** First substantial element after starting, before any content
**Key Quote:** "I want them to judge me based on what I'm promising... this is my big promise"

### Tonality and Energy Imperative
**Node:** 3.8 Energy Calibration
**Core Focus:** Level 10 energy with extreme vocal variation throughout
**When to Use:** Entire webinar, especially critical when attendees can't see you
**Key Quote:** "Your tonality and your voice is what keeps people around"

## Hook Architecture (Tier 3)

### The End Hook
**Node:** 3.1 Hook Architecture
**Core Focus:** Promise exclusive value only at very end of webinar
**When to Use:** Within first 5-7 minutes, immediately after bold promise
**Key Quote:** "If you stay to the end, I'm gonna do something really, really cool"

### The First 5-Minute Retention Window
**Node:** 3.1 Hook Architecture
**Core Focus:** 90% of leavers exit in first 4-5 minutes - this is THE battle
**When to Use:** When planning introduction and understanding critical retention moment
**Key Quote:** "90% of your people will leave, those that are gonna leave... You can get past that first little hump, they're gonna stay the entire 90 minutes"

### The 10-Year Compression Promise
**Node:** 3.1 Hook Architecture
**Core Focus:** Position system as decades compressed into days/hours
**When to Use:** When framing value proposition of your teaching
**Key Quote:** "I wanna just shortcut you guys and just give you the template... the first time out of the gate, or maybe not the first, close to the first time you can have the perfect webinar"

### The Stay-To-End Value Stack
**Node:** 3.1 Hook Architecture
**Core Focus:** Make end hook exclusive, valuable, and worth waiting for
**When to Use:** In first 5-7 minutes when setting up end hook
**Key Quote:** "You only get that if you stay 'til the end"

## Authority Establishment (Tier 3-4)

### Qualification Stack (Tier 3)
**Node:** 3.2 Authority Establishment
**Core Focus:** Build credibility through specific proof points
**When to Use:** After commanding attention, before transitioning to content
**Key Quote:** "I don't talk about this a lot, but I'm the king of high ticket sales... There's nobody in the marketplace better than me at high ticket sales"

### The Hard Way vs Cheat Method (Tier 4)
**Node:** 3.2 Authority Establishment
**Core Focus:** "I struggled so you can have the shortcut"
**When to Use:** When establishing authority and positioning your teaching
**Key Quote:** "I discovered the hard way, but you're gonna have a chance to cheat"

### The Pattern Interrupt Within Qualification (Tier 4)
**Node:** 3.2 Authority Establishment
**Core Focus:** Insert humor/unexpected element before authority claim
**When to Use:** When qualifying yourself to avoid sounding like pure self-promotion
**Key Quote:** "I sort of picture me sleeping, just to be funny, kind of a pattern interrupt and then I came back and said, look... I'm the king of high ticket sales"

## Energy Calibration (Tier 3)

### Pre-Start Energy Loop
**Node:** 3.8 Energy Calibration
**Core Focus:** Maintain level 10 energy in 2-4 minutes before start and first few after
**When to Use:** In the 2-4 minutes before webinar starts and first few minutes after start time
**Key Quote:** "I like getting on and just saying, hey everyone... And I keep looping that thing, 'cause people are always calling in"

### The Continuous Energy Arc
**Node:** 3.8 Energy Calibration
**Core Focus:** Energy cannot dip at any point for full 90-120 minutes
**When to Use:** Throughout webinar from pre-start through close
**Key Quote:** "So I'm like at a level 10 right now... I'm literally sitting on the edge of my seat. I have my hands in front of me and I'm moving them around"

## Frame Setting & Attention (Tier 4)

### Command Attention
**Node:** 3.7 Frame Setting
**Core Focus:** Direct instruction to eliminate specific distractions
**When to Use:** After bold promise and end hook, before qualification
**Key Quote:** "If you got your cell phone out, if you got Skype, Facebook, you gotta close it down, we need you, I need your attention"

**When to Use:** After qualification, transitioning from intro to content
**Key Quote:** "Can you imagine that? Making high ticket sales out every afternoon and pick up the phone ever. Like what would you do with your life?"
