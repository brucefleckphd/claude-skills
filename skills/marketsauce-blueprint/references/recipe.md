## SECTION I: MASTER BUYER'S BRIEF

### Start Here: Market Analysis

Act as a world-class marketer and customer researcher specializing in marketing psychology and human behavior.

Analyze the [Segment] for [Product/Service] based on [Context]:

**Geographic:** Where are they located?

**Demographic:** 
- B2B: Job roles, industry, company size/type
- B2C: Age, sex, income, profession

**Psychographic:** How do they think? What do they need? What do they appreciate?

**Behavioral:** [Needs], [Wants], [Desires], [Behaviors], [Pain Points]

Go deep on psychosocial elements driving this segment. Articulate visceral feelings and emotional driving behaviors. Focus on the HUMAN, not the product. Bold each bullet header and expand with comprehensive insight.

---

### Goals

#### Primary Goal
Create 1 [Primary Goal] for [Segment] related to [Niche]. Use future tense verb. This is the "big picture" thing the product/service helps achieve.

**Primary Goal Topic:** List 10 well-researched topic ideas (1-2 words max) related to the primary goal.

#### Primary Complaints
The most pressing concerns related to this niche/offer. List 10 examples using visceral emotional language.

**Primary Complaint Topics:** 10 broad terms (1-2 words max)

#### Secondary Complaints
Other nagging problems related to the primary complaint. List 10 examples using visceral emotional language.

**Secondary Complaint Topics:** 10 broad terms (1-2 words max)

#### Secondary Goals
Additional goals directly associated with the primary goal. List 10 important secondary goals.

**Secondary Goal Topics:** 10 broad terms (1-2 words max)

#### Goal Descriptors
10 micro-promise adverbs that would be most compelling based on the primary goal.

Create 1-sentence positioning statements leveraging each goal descriptor.

#### Positive Feelings
10 super-short expressions of what the customer really wants in their life relative to the offer.

---

### Psychosocial Deep-Dive

#### Dreams
The "Gigantic Why" - why they want to achieve goals in this niche. Top 10 dreams related to primary goal.

#### Objections
Things they refuse to do to experience results (ideally things they don't have to do with this offer). Top 10 objections to buying.

#### Negative Feelings
10 current negative feelings about not achieving their primary goal.

#### Ultimate Fear
Worst-case scenario if primary goal goes unfulfilled. 10 negative consequences.

#### Promises
Benefits, tips, tactics, and learning the offer provides. 10 specific promises related to results.

---

### Causes

#### Mistaken Beliefs > The Truth Is
10 beliefs prospects hold as reasons they can't achieve the primary goal.
After each, respond with "The truth is..."

#### The Enemy
Sources of misinformation about the problem:
- 10 authoritative sources that mislead (Format: "1. [Source]")
- 10 things friends/family/colleagues say (Format: "1. 'Friend's Name', a trusted source, says...")
- 10 ideas keeping prospects from seeing solutions (Format: "1. Prospects think/believe...")
- 10 things the internet says (Format: "1. A quick Google search for...")

#### Cause Triggers
Factors that "cause the cause" - 10 causes (2-3 words) with explanation of why.
10 possible resolutions with detailed explanations (5-10 sentences each, practical tips for quick wins).

#### Primary Cause Defined
Detailed explanation of the primary cause and why it prevents achieving goals. Use specific relatable examples.

#### Consequences
10 negative consequences of not achieving primary goal.
For each, write a short paragraph using visceral emotional language demonstrating how it negatively affects life.

#### Negative Statistics
Frightening statistics about those who fail to achieve the primary goal. Include citations from credible sources.

#### Bad Habits
Behaviors that prevent success. List common bad habits of this avatar.

---

### Solutions

#### False Solutions
Things tried that failed:
- Part 1: "Many [Avatars] are trying [False Solution], and it rarely works."
- Part 2: What is the result of the false solution? Real-world examples.
- Part 3: Better alternative using creative storytelling.

#### Success Myths
False beliefs about what successful people do that this avatar can't do.

#### Topic Challenges
- 10 physical problems (1-3 words)
- 10 emotional problems related to physical
- 10 psychological problems related to physical/emotional
- 10 spiritual problems related to all above

For each challenge, describe viable solution using creative storytelling.

#### Expensive Alternatives
10 costly options with their drawbacks.

### Offers

10 benefits in with/without format.

#### Offer Reframe
10 reframes: "It isn't just ____, it's _____."
