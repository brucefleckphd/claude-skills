## Headlines

**12 frameworks for writing attention-grabbing headlines**

### Tier 1 (Master Systems)
- **The 100 Headlines Method** - Quantity produces quality; write 100, 10% will be good
- **The Opposite Headline Technique** - Do the opposite for breakthrough angles
- **The Big Idea Headline** - Change how they think about the problem

### Tier 2 (Core Frameworks)
- **The Fantasy Headline Method** - "If I could say anything, what would it be?"
- **The Crazy Attention Headline** - Start with interesting unrelated thing
- **The Body on the Floor Opener** - Start at peak drama
- **The Add/Subtract/Divide Method** - Systematic variations
- **The Mechanism Headline** - HOW you do it for mature markets

### Tier 3 (Tactical)
- **The Cliffhanger Headline** - Adapt proven templates
- **The Counterintuitive Headline** - Say opposite of expectation
- **The Simplicity Principle** - Caveman simple wins
- **Headlines from Swipe File Study** - Take concepts, not words

## Workflow Sequences

### Complete Sales Letter
1. `deutsch-strategy` (4 C's, market sophistication, big idea)
2. `deutsch-psychology` (identify emotional triggers)
3. `deutsch-leads` (dual journey, resistance-free)
4. `deutsch-body` + `deutsch-stories` (energy, people doing stuff)
5. `deutsch-bullets` (polishing method)
6. `deutsch-close` (crossroads, future pace)

### Quick Copy Review
1. 4 C's check (Clear? Compelling? Credible? Change?)
2. 2 H's check (Honest? Heart-to-heart?)
3. Dual journey check (Both intellectual AND emotional?)
4. Energy audit (Find best paragraph, bring others up)

### Headline Session
1. `deutsch-headlines` (100 method, opposites)
2. Market sophistication check
3. Big idea verification

### Story Enhancement
1. Identify which of 3 story types needed
2. Apply Hero's Journey structure
3. Inject conflict
4. TV show test
