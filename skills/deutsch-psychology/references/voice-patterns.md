# David Deutsch Voice Patterns - Psychology

## Psychology-Specific Voice Guidance

Use this reference when applying Deutsch's buyer psychology principles.

---

## THE DEUTSCH PSYCHOLOGY VOICE

### Core Principle
People act from fear MORE than desire. Safety comes first. Then show them the transformation they can become.

### The Hierarchy
"The first thing that we all want is to be safe. Fear beats greed."

---

## VOICE PATTERNS BY EMOTIONAL TRIGGER

### 1. Fear Voice
Activate primal survival:
- "The danger nobody's warning you about"
- "What happens if you do nothing"
- "You're losing [X] every day you wait"
- "Before it's too late"

**Tone:** Urgent, protective, concerned for them

### 2. Gain Voice (What Money BRINGS)
Not just money - what money represents:
- "The freedom to..."
- "Finally take that trip..."
- "Take care of your family the way..."
- "Prove to your brother-in-law..."

**Tone:** Dreamy, specific pictures, emotional payoffs

### 3. Ease Voice
Appeal to fundamental laziness:
- "Without lifting a finger"
- "In just minutes"
- "Practically does itself"
- "While you sleep"

**Tone:** Effortless, automatic, no work required

### 4. Edge/Advantage Voice
Secret knowledge power:
- "What they don't want you to know"
- "The insider secret that..."
- "Get there before everyone else"
- "The unfair advantage"

**Tone:** Exclusive, empowering, ahead of others

### 5. Absolution Voice
Remove blame and guilt:
- "It's not your fault"
- "You were lied to"
- "Nobody told you because..."
- "The real culprit is..."

**Tone:** Understanding, validating, exonerating

### 6. Insider Status Voice
Exclusive club membership:
- "For the select few who..."
- "Inner circle access"
- "What members know"
- "Invitation only"

**Tone:** Elite, exclusive, belonging

### 7. Freedom Voice
Liberation from control:
- "Free yourself from..."
- "No longer dependent on..."
- "On your own terms"
- "Break free from..."

**Tone:** Independent, self-reliant, autonomous

### 8. Recognition Voice
Being seen and acknowledged:
- "You deserve..."
- "Finally get the credit..."
- "Others will notice..."
- "Be the one people ask..."

**Tone:** Validating, elevating, acknowledging worth

### 9. Payback/Revenge Voice
Proving doubters wrong:
- "Show them they were wrong"
- "Get the last laugh"
- "While they're still struggling"
- "Who's laughing now"

**Tone:** Vindictive but justified, satisfying

### 10. Transformation Voice
Becoming a NEW person:
- "Become the person who..."
- "A completely different life"
- "Everything changes"
- "You'll never be the same"

**Tone:** Profound, identity-shifting, evolutionary

## SPECIAL PSYCHOLOGY PATTERNS

### The Touch of Larceny
Almost illegal feeling:
- "How to Rob Banks" (legally!)
- "This is almost unfair"
- "Before they shut this down"
- "Getting away with..."

**Voice:** Slightly mischievous, exciting, edge-of-legal

### The Real Benefit Chain
Feature → Benefit → Emotion → Life:
- Newsletter → Make money → Trip to Disneyland → Memories with grandkids → Being THAT grandparent

**Voice:** Drill deeper and deeper to true desire

### The Money Isn't Money Insight
Above $75K, it's about meaning:
- Freedom to choose
- Proving doubters wrong
- Taking care of family
- Status and recognition

**Voice:** Understand what money REPRESENTS

## PSYCHOLOGY CHECKLIST

- [ ] Lead emotion identified (fear beats greed)
- [ ] At least 3 triggers activated
- [ ] Absolution offered where appropriate
- [ ] Transformation promise present
- [ ] Real benefits, not just features
- [ ] Touch of larceny where appropriate
- [ ] Freedom emphasized over money
- [ ] Recognition included

---

## SEQUENCING PSYCHOLOGY

### Fear → Transformation Sequence
1. Activate fear (what happens if they don't act)
2. Offer absolution (not your fault)
3. Show edge/advantage (here's the secret)
4. Paint transformation (become new person)
5. Remove risk (safe to act)

### Frustration → Vindication Sequence
1. Acknowledge frustration (tried everything)
2. Absolution (wasn't your fault)
3. Reveal truth (here's why it didn't work)
4. New path (here's what will)
5. Revenge/payback (prove them wrong)
