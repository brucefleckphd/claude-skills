## Master Systems (Tier 1)

### The 10 Emotional Triggers
- **File:** `psychology-10-emotional-triggers.yml`
- **Use when:** Planning any copy; need emotional angle
- **Covers:** Fear, Gain, Ease, Edge, Absolution, Insider Status, Freedom, Recognition, Revenge, Transformation
- **Pairs with:** deutsch-headlines, deutsch-leads, deutsch-close

### The Transformation Imperative
- **File:** `psychology-transformation-imperative.yml`
- **Use when:** Showing results; closing
- **Core:** Don't promise improvement - promise TRANSFORMATION into a different person
- **Pairs with:** psychology-real-benefit-chain, psychology-freedom-framework

### Fear Beats Greed
- **File:** `psychology-fear-beats-greed.yml`
- **Use when:** Choosing lead emotion
- **Core:** People act from fear MORE than desire. Safety comes before gain.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-leads

### The Guarantee Presentation
- **File:** `psychology-guarantee-presentation.yml`
- **Use when:** Writing guarantee copy
- **Core:** Never negative ("If you hate it..."), always positive ("You've got to love it...")
- **Pairs with:** deutsch-close, psychology-risk-transfer

## Core Frameworks (Tier 2)

### The Touch of Larceny
- **File:** `psychology-touch-of-larceny.yml`
- **Use when:** Need to make offer feel exciting, exclusive
- **Core:** People love feeling like they're getting away with something. Almost illegal. Almost unfair.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-almost-unfair-edge, psychology-insider-status

### The Freedom Framework
- **File:** `psychology-freedom-framework.yml`
- **Use when:** People want money or health for its own sake
- **Core:** They don't want money - they want FREEDOM. Feature → Benefit → Ultimate Freedom Benefit
- **Pairs with:** psychology-real-benefit-chain, psychology-transformation-imperative

### The Revenge Motivator
- **File:** `psychology-revenge-motivator.yml`
- **Use when:** Audience has been told they'll fail; doubted
- **Core:** People want to prove doubters wrong. Parents, teachers, colleagues.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-stories

### The Real Benefit Chain
- **File:** `psychology-real-benefit-chain.yml`
- **Use when:** Benefits feel weak
- **Pattern:** Feature → Benefit → Emotional Benefit → Life Transformation
- **Pairs with:** psychology-freedom-framework, psychology-transformation-imperative

### The Absolution Pattern
- **File:** `psychology-absolution-pattern.yml`
- **Use when:** Audience feels like failures
- **Core:** It's not your fault. The government, bad advice, hidden information - THEY'RE to blame.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-leads

### The Recognition Appeal
- **File:** `psychology-recognition-appeal.yml`
- **Use when:** Audience wants to be seen, acknowledged
- **Techniques:** Recognize in copy, show how product brings recognition, feature customers
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-body

### The Insider Status
- **File:** `psychology-insider-status.yml`
- **Use when:** Need exclusivity angle
- **Core:** Make product feel like membership in exclusive club with insider knowledge
- **Pairs with:** psychology-touch-of-larceny, psychology-recognition-appeal

### The Money Isn't Money Insight
- **File:** `psychology-money-isnt-money.yml`
- **Use when:** Writing for make-money audiences
- **Core:** Above $75K, money doesn't buy more happiness. They want what it REPRESENTS.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-freedom-framework, psychology-real-benefit-chain

## Tactical (Tier 3)

### The Overweight Shame Insight
- **File:** `psychology-overweight-shame.yml`
- **Use when:** Writing for weight loss
- **Core:** Overweight people feel watched and judged constantly. In restaurants, on planes, everywhere.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-stories

### The "Almost Unfair" Edge
- **File:** `psychology-almost-unfair-edge.yml`
- **Use when:** Positioning offer
- **Core:** Make them feel like they're getting an unfair advantage. Almost too good. But not.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-touch-of-larceny, psychology-insider-status

### The Ease Appeal
- **File:** `psychology-ease-appeal.yml`
- **Use when:** Showing benefit
- **Core:** People don't want to work. If you make their lives easier, that's a tremendous benefit.
- **Pairs with:** psychology-10-emotional-triggers, deutsch-bullets
