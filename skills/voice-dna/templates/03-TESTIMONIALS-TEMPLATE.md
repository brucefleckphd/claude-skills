## Testimonial Usage Guide

### For Sales Pages
**Best to use**: Tier 1 testimonials with specific results, quantified impact
**Format**: Use real name, title, company
**Length**: 1-2 sentences max
**Why it works**: Credibility + specificity

---

### For Email Sequences
**Best to use**: Tier 2 testimonials, thematic grouping
**Format**: Pull short 1-sentence quotes, can be anonymous
**Length**: Single powerful sentence
**Why it works**: Breaks up email content, adds proof points

---

### For Social Media
**Best to use**: Tier 3 testimonials, short and punchy
**Format**: Can be anonymous, focus on emotional benefit
**Length**: Tweet-length (280 chars)
**Why it works**: Engaging, varied proof

---

### For Webinars/Speaking
**Best to use**: Tier 1 testimonials, detailed results
**Format**: Real name, title, specific story
**Length**: Full quote allowed
**Why it works**: Live credibility, personal connection
